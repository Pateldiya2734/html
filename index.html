ABOUT INDUS UNIVERSITY

Indus University (Gujarat) was established in 2012 under the Gujarat State Private Universities Act and is approved by BCI, UGC, AICTE, and COA. Indus University Ahmedabad, through 10 constituent colleges, offers UG, PG, PhD, certificate, and other courses to s tudents across Management, Science, Architecture, and various other streams. The Indus University Gujarat is accredited by NAAC.
