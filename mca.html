Masters of Computer Application

What is Masters of Computer Application (MCA)?

MCA is a postgraduate course that trains students in the various aspects of computer programs, application software, computer architecture, operating systems and many more. The MCA Course duration is 2 years.

    MCA Eligibility

  • Candidates should have passed their 10+2 in Science stream having Mathematics as the compulsory subject.
  • Aspirants must have completed their bachelor's with at least 55% of the minimum aggregate from a recognized University
  • Aspirants should complete their bachelor's degree in any relevant discipline such as BTech, BCA, BSc, or other fields.
  • One must also need to qualify for an entrance exam conducted at the National/State or University level and may be required to face a Group discussion or personal interview round.
